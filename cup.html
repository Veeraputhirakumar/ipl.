IPL Title Winners

2008

Rajasthan Royals

In this 20 over the game, Rajasthan Royals had won by three wickets by chasing a score of 163 made by Chennai super kings.

2009

Deccan Charges

the Chargers had won the game after defending a score of 143 by six runs. It was an entertaining match in which Challengers made 137 with the loss of 9 wickets in 20 overs.

Chennai Super Kings

The kings were able to defend their score of 205 by 58 runs against the DL Vettori led team.

Sunrisers Hyderabad

The Sunrisers had scored 208 for the loss of 7 wickets in 20 overs and had won by eight runs. This match was played at M.Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru, on the night of 29th May 2016.

2017

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians had made 129 for the loss of eight wickets while Rising Pune Supergiant had scored 128 at the loss of 6 wickets

Chennai Super Kings

Mahendra Singh Dhoni the team had scored 181 for the loss of 2 wickets with nine balls in hand won against SRH

2019

Mumbai Indians

2020

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians won the IPL 2020 Final by 5 wickets with 8 balls remaining and Trent Boult became the Man of the Match.

2021

Chennai Super Kings

CSK won the IPL 2021 Final by 27 runs for the 4th time. Faf du Plessis was awarded Man of the Match in the IPL 2021 Final.

2022

Gujurat Titans

Gujurat Titans playing their first tournament, won the match and the title by defeating Rajasthan Royals by seven wickets. Captain of the Titans, Hardik Pandya, was also the player of the match
